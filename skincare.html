Threading

Threading is temporal hair removal.It helps to enhance the facial features.It is 100% natural.Most young women were introduced to the salon with the need to thread their eyebrows, chin, upper lip.

Even though it is one of the most basic of the salon services, at our Salon every staff is trained and experienced to do the very basic yet very important service with care and attention for detail.

Waxing

Waxing is the process of hair removal from the root by using a covering of a sticky subtance,such as wax to adhere to body hair and then removing this covering and pulling out the hair from follicle.The best known benefit of waxing is that the hair doesn't grow back very quickly.As a result,with waxing you get an incredibly smooth finish.

You can opt for a basic Honey wax or choose the flavor you most like from our range of premium waxes.

Detan

We all know that during the summer season the sun is brutal and very harsh on the skin.As you step out in the sun,there are almost 70% chances of getting sun-tan and sun-burn,even if you have applied any number of cream packs.

What is Tanning?
In simple words tanning is the process in which the skin pigment melalin is increased due to the overexposure to the sunlight.This is the natural way of protecting the skin from sun damage.Face,neck,arms,hands,feet are some of the areas that are more prone to suntan.

What is De tan ?
Detan is the processes of removing the pile-up of cells and skin which is tanned due to the exposure to pollution radiation emitted by the sun.Please note that it is not an alternative to get fairer skin,but it is done to remove the tanned skin to counterbalance the skin tone.

Hand & Feet

A manicure is a cosmetic beauty treatment for the fingernails and hands performed at home or in a salon. It is an essential part of good grooming.

A pedicure is a superficial cosmetic treatment of the feet and toenails.Pedicure means care of feet and toenails.Feet bear the burden of our entire body so we should not neglect them.
