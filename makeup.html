Make up

What is the purpose of MAKEup?

Makeup mainly is used to change or enhance the way we look, to feel more confident and also to hide our imperfections. Makeup can be termed as a cosmetic device that is used to prettify or add color to your face Makeup can also make a person look more presentable and give off the impression of self-care. You can use makeup to look best in photos. Sometimes the natural features on your face will not be all that noticeable. But with makeup, you can add a brighter look to your face and produce a good style that adds a nice touch all around and helps you to look your best for the camera.

Consult with an expert to get the best of make-up services. Pick and choose your make-up needs and we'll be there to spin magic on your Big Day.

Makeup helps you to protect your skin and keep it safe from pollution, smog etc that harm your skin. Makeup creates a slight barrier around your skin to keep it from being directly exposed to dust. While makeup is not going to offer total protection, the risk of being harmed will at least remain minimal.

Day Makeup

The purpose of daytime makeup is often to highlight natural features, conceal flaws and blemishes in the skin, and promote a fresh and awake appearance. Daytime makeup looks are generally subtler than evening or nighttime looks, because the natural light of the sun makes heavy makeup jobs more apparent

Evening Makeup/Party Makeup

Evening makeup usually differs from daytime makeup in depth and color.The smoky eye look, seen here, is very popular for nigh.Evening makeup usually differs from daytime makeup in depth and color. Engagement Makeup

The ambience in which you will have to walk up to the stage to exchange rings with your beloved and make it an official statement for all to witness and cherish a forever story, is very significant in helping you decide which engagement makeup you prefer.

Engagement Makeup neither as light as party makeup nor as heavy as bridal makeup.your makeup entirely depends on your outfit. So the first thing you need to do is, decide on an outfit according to the theme.

Bridal/Reception Makeup

A wedding ceremony is one of the most memorable occasions in anyone's life. That is why people tend to unleash their 'perfectionist' sides when it comes to planning weddings. A perfect wedding requires each aspect of it to be amazing. Apart from the venue, the décor, ambiance, and bride's dresses, people also pay a lot of attention to the bride's makeup. It is what gives the bride's outfit a finishing look.Bridal makeup is something that you cannot overlook, especially in an Indian wedding. Bridal makeup is an essential element in any Indian wedding. It is what gives the bride a complete look and the indistinguishable quality that the star of the evening needs to possess. Therefore, bridal makeup needs to be on point – beautiful but in a very subtle and artistic manner.

Bridal/Reception Makeup is extensive with different techniques like HD, airbrush and mineral, is used to get the right kind of makeup. It requires special products and tools to apply. The makeup is heavier than party or engagement makeup to have a long-lasting effect since you’ll be sitting through your wedding for a long time.
