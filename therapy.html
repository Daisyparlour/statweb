Aroma Therapy

Aroma therapy is the controlled therapeutic use of pure essential oils(extracts from plants) to enhance psychological and physical well-being to gives deep relaxation pain and tension. Essential oils are made from flower, herb, and tree parts like bark, roots, peels, and petals. The cells that give a plant its fragrant smell are its "essence." When an essence is extracted from a plant, it becomes an essential oil.

If you need improvement in health problems from anxiety to poor sleep, you may want to consider aromatherapy by either breathing them through your nose or putting them on your skin. Some people put the oils on their skin when they get a massage or take a bath.
