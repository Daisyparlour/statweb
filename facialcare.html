Facials

The skin is the largest organ in the integumentary system of the body.The skin absorbs oxygen,carbon dioxide and nitrogen in small amounts. It also absorbs many of the chemicals that are in the envirnoment such as pollutants and certain chemicals from body care and cleaning products.

The first and most important part of a facial is the correct diagnosis of the skin type. This is carried out at the beginning of each facial.The beauty therapist choose the correct skin care products and facial treatments for the clients skin type.This assessment is called skin analysis.The skin can be devided in to various types according to the texture.

Treatment

The skin is subjected to various problems due to external or internal causes.Most of them if neglected,can become persistent and develop in to serious disorders.some skin disorders can be treated with and under the supervision of a physician.

If a client has a skin condition that the cosmetologist does not recognize as a simple disorder,the person should be reffered to a physician.

Cosmetic Electrology

Cosmetic electrotherapy is a range of beauty treatments that uses low electric currents passed through the skin to produce several therapeutic effects such as muscle toning in the body and micro-lifting of the face.
